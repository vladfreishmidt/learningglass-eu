Why the Learning Glass Works for Education:

The learning glass is a wonderful educational tool. Tailor made for education it was created by Professor, Matt Anderson to better engage with and enhance his students learning experience.

The learning glass is used for creating content online, both for recorded materials for students to review at their own pace and live streaming to classes when it is not possible for them to be there in person.

Find out more about how the learning glass works here!

  • By keeping the teacher's face facing the student at all times they are more engaging, which students respond extremely positively and have an adhanced learning experienced.
  • Capturing student attention is key to effective teaching and the unique and fun style of the learning glass does this as students enjoy the bright colours and writing with light.
  • They are easy to use so people with less technical ability can create high quality content on their own
  • The design of the smaller learning glass means it can be easily moved around and can be used in a teacher’s home.
  • Students can access recordings of materials and replay areas they don’t understand straight away or in revision, meaning teachers don’t have to repeat content and students have access to information whenever they need it.
