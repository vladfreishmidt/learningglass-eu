Why the Learning Glass Works for Education:

The learning glass is a wonderful educational tool. Tailor made for education it was created by Professor, Matt Anderson to better engage his students and enhance their learning experience.

The learning glass is used for creating interactive online learning content. This can be in the form of producing recorded materials for students to review at their own pace and for live streaming to classes during remote schooling.

Find out more about how the learning glass works here!

  • Capturing student’s attention is key to effective teaching. The learning glass is perfect for this as students enjoy it’s unique and fun style and the effect of the neon illumination.
  • When presenting with the Learning Glass Teachers continuously face their students and write as they speak. This is proven to better engage students, improve their learning experience and increase retention of information.
  • The Learning Glass is easy to use regardless of your technical ability, meaning all Teachers can create high quality content on their own.
  • The design of the small learning glass means it can be easily moved, shared and can be setup on any table at a teacher’s home.
  • Students can access recordings of materials and replay areas they don’t understand or want to repeat. This works well for revision purposes, or for students with additional needs as it ensures students have access to information at their own pace, whenever they need it.
