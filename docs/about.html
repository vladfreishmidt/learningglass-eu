About Learning Glass Europe

We established Learning Glass Europe to build on the incredible success of Learning Glass Corporation in the Unites States and to enable European Schools, Universities, and businesses to benefit from this unique learning technology.

Initially, we launched the product in the UK via our partner, Talking Edge Studios, based in London. Talking Edge have also been delivering a lightboard video production service to businesses for the last 18 months, however, this was severely disrupted when COVID became widespread.

When the education sector was shut down, very quickly the switch or ‘pivot’ was made to focus on helping Universities to deliver online learning with all the benefits available from the lightboard.

It quickly became clear, that there was a widespread urgency to solve this problem across the entire education sector in Europe and so we established Learning Glass Europe in the Netherlands and appointed a Business Development Manager in Munich, Germany. At this point we have sales and marketing capability in the UK, Germany, and the Netherlands.

Dr. Matthew Anderson – Founder of Learning Glass USA (Non-Exec Director)

Dr Matt Anderson is the designer of the original learning glass and Founder of Learning Glass US. He has over 25 years experience in Physics research and Education. Ronald Huisman works as an associate Professor at the Erasmus School of Economics. He has won teaching awards from different institutes as a result of his innovative teaching methods applied to courses about financial economics, energy finance, and data analytics. Besides his academic live, Ronald is an entrepreneur. He lives in The Hague, the Netherlands. He is bringing his passion for education, innovation and entrepreneurship to ensure the success of Learning Glass Europe.
