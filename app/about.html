About Learning Glass Europe

We established Learning Glass Europe to build on the incredible success of Learning Glass Corporation in the Unites States and to enable European Schools, Universities, and businesses to benefit from this unique learning technology.

nitially, we launched the product in the UK via our partner, Talking Edge Studios, based in London. Talking Edge have also been delivering a lightboard video production service to businesses for the last 18 months, however, this was severely disrupted when COVID became widespread.

When the education sector was shut down, very quickly the switch or ‘pivot’ was made to focus on helping Universities to deliver online learning with all the benefits available from the lightboard.

It quickly became clear, that there was a widespread urgency to solve this problem across the entire education sector in Europe and so we established Learning Glass Europe in the Netherlands and appointed a Business Development Manager in Munich, Germany. At this point we have sales and marketing capability in the UK, Germany, and the Netherlands.
