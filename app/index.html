The Learning Glass Difference

Invented by a Physics Professor

There's nothing more engaging than talking face-to-face. That is why Learning Glass is such a powerful tool for your video presentations. Lightboard technology enables your viewers -- students, colleagues, training participants -- to learn from you as if you were in the same room. There are is no new technology for instructors to master, they simply write on the glass as if they were using a whiteboard.

Explore Product Line
 

How It Works

  • LED lighting on specialised glass creates a transparent white board that illuminates writing with neon markers.
  • Two sheets with LED lights focused down through the airgap.
  • Light gets 'trapped' and 'escapes' through the neon markings
  • Black backdrop and specialist lighting set-up to eliminate reflections
  • Image is 'flipped' in post-production
